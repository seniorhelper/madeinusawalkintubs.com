Expert Safety Insights & FAQ

What makes a tub "USA-Made" vs "USA-Assembled"? +
A true USA-made walk-in tub uses American-sourced acrylic and components, manufactured in domestic facilities under US labor laws and quality standards. Aging Safely Baths prioritizes these units because they offer superior durability and longer warranties compared to imported alternatives.
How does the VA HISA Grant work for walk-in tubs? +
The Home Improvement and Structural Alterations (HISA) grant provides medically necessary improvements to a Veteran’s primary residence. We provide the detailed, itemized quotes required by the VA to help veterans secure funding for their safety modifications.
What is the typical timeframe for installation? +
Most professional installations are completed within 1 to 3 days. Our white-glove service includes the removal of your old tub, plumbing upgrades, and the precision fitting of your new walk-in system, followed by a full safety demonstration.
